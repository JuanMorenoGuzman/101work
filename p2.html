Project 2

For this project I wanted to create something elegant and not too colorful due to everything I have made so far being like that. I used one example that was provided for us (black/white binking lines in background) I modified it and I went from there. I then added my class "Sprinkles" which are the lines going across the screen. And lastly I was having difficulty getting what I wanted in the middle but after watching a tutorial and modifying the code I ended up with what I wanted. The blinking/changing lines in the background achieve this through the use of millis, the white lines going across are repeating arrays and the circles moving at the center use sin and cos methods to achieve their movement. The algorthm being used is the NoiseWave, which kind of acts as water and the circles as a fish that jumps out from it then goes back.
